Lurie Real Estate Strategies focuses on facilitating multi-family, single family residential, and commercial developments inside The Perimeter or close to popular activity centers in Metro Atlanta. Bob Lurie works with clients to acquire land or properties suited to adaptive reuse that may or may not be listed for sale and to negotiate these deals to ensure the best possible outcomes for all parties.

Bob combines his experience in architecture, real estate brokerage, development and consulting to help guide his clients through the land buying and selling processes. Because of his broad skill set and experience, Bob brings a keen insights to the process of acquiring land. Whether it's locating land for sale in an in-demand area, identifying a not-yet-listed parcel that closely fits a client's needs, or representing a landowner in the search for a qualified buyer, his clients can have confidence that they are receiving honest, informed advice, reliable and responsive service, and the kind of personal attention that can only be found at a boutique real estate firm.

As the head of Lurie Real Estate Strategies, Bob offers the following services to his clients:

  • Commercial Real Estate Brokerage and Sales
  • Financial Modeling
  • Market Research
  • Development Management
  • Consulting and Bad Deal Avoidance
